Italy

Pizza

Classic Italian pizza with various toppings.

Pasta

Different types like spaghetti, fettuccine, and ravioli.

Gelato

Italian ice cream with rich and creamy flavors.

Lasagna

Layered pasta with meat, cheese, and sauce.

Cannoli

Fried pastry filled with sweet ricotta cheese.

Risotto

Creamy rice dish often flavored with saffron and seafood.

Prosciutto

Thinly sliced cured ham, often served with melon or bread.

Frittata

Italian-style omelette filled with vegetables or meats.

Panettone

Sweet Italian bread loaf, typically enjoyed at Christmas.

Porchetta

Roasted pork with a crispy, flavorful skin, often served in sandwiches.

Arancini

Fried rice balls stuffed with cheese, meat, or vegetables.

Bruschetta

Grilled bread topped with tomatoes, basil, and olive oil.

Korea

Bibimbap

Rice mixed with vegetables, meat, egg, and spicy gochujang sauce.

Kimchi

Fermented vegetables, usually cabbage, with chili and garlic.

Ramyeon

Korean instant noodles served with broth and toppings.

Bulgogi

Grilled marinated beef with a sweet and savory sauce.

Kimchi Jjigae

A spicy stew made with kimchi, tofu, and pork or tuna.

Tteokbokki

Spicy stir-fried rice cakes in a sweet and savory sauce.

Jeon

Korean savory pancakes made with vegetables or seafood.

Samgyeopsal

Grilled pork belly served with lettuce, garlic, and ssamjang.

Hoddeok

Sweet pancakes filled with brown sugar, cinnamon, and nuts.

Galbitang

Beef short rib soup served with a light broth and rice.

Soondubu

Soft tofu stew with seafood or meat, served with spicy broth.

Japchae

Stir-fried glass noodles with vegetables, often including beef or pork.

India

Butter Chicken

A creamy and flavorful chicken curry.

Samosa

Fried pastry filled with spiced potatoes and peas.

Chole Bhature

Spicy chickpea curry with deep-fried bread.

Biryani

Aromatic rice dish with meat, spices, and herbs.

Palak Paneer

Cottage cheese in a smooth spinach gravy.

Dosa

Crispy rice crepe served with chutneys and sambar.

Rogan Josh

Slow-cooked lamb in a rich and aromatic gravy.

Daal Tadka

Spiced lentils cooked with tempering of garlic, cumin, and ghee.

Pav Bhaji

Spicy mashed vegetable curry served with buttered bread rolls.

Khichdi

A comforting dish made with rice and lentils.

Seekh Kebab

Minced meat skewers spiced with aromatic herbs and grilled.

Gulab Jamun

Sweet, deep-fried dough balls soaked in sugar syrup.

France

Baguette

Classic French long loaf bread, often served with cheese or butter.

Croissant

Buttery, flaky pastry often enjoyed at breakfast.

Coq au Vin

Chicken braised in red wine with mushrooms, onions, and garlic.

Ratatouille

Vegetable stew with eggplant, zucchini, and tomatoes.

Escargots de Bourgogne

Snails cooked in garlic, butter, and parsley.

Quiche Lorraine

A savory pie with eggs, cheese, and bacon.

Bouillabaisse

Seafood stew from Marseille, made with fish, shellfish, and vegetables.

Duck Confit

Slow-cooked duck legs crispy on the outside and tender on the inside.

Tarte Tatin

An upside-down caramelized apple tart.

Crêpes

Thin pancakes filled with a variety of sweet or savory ingredients.

French Onion Soup

Caramelized onions in broth, topped with cheese and toasted bread.

Tarte Aux Fruits

Classic French fruit tart made with fresh seasonal fruits and pastry cream.

Russia

Borscht

Traditional beet soup served hot or cold, often with sour cream and dill.

Blini

Thin pancakes served with sour cream, caviar, or jam.

Pelmeni

Russian dumplings filled with minced meat and served with butter or sour cream.

Beef Stroganoff

Sauteed beef in a creamy sauce, often served with mashed potatoes or rice.

Pirozhki

Small pastries filled with various ingredients like meat, cabbage, or potatoes.

Kholodets

Jellied meat dish made from pork or beef, often served cold with horseradish.

Shchi

Traditional Russian cabbage soup, often served with sour cream and rye bread.

Solyanka

A hearty, sour soup made with a mix of meats, vegetables, and brine.

Kvass

A fermented beverage made from rye bread, with a mildly alcoholic content.

Caviar

Fish roe, often served with blini, butter, or crackers.

Vareniki

Dumplings filled with various fillings like potatoes, cherries, or cheese.

Kissel

A sweet fruit-based drink or dessert, often thickened with starch.

Switzerland

Fondue

Classic Swiss dish of melted cheese served with bread, potatoes, or vegetables for dipping.

Rösti

Swiss-style crispy potato dish, often served for breakfast or as a side.

Swiss Chocolate

Famous for its high quality, Swiss chocolate is a delicious treat loved worldwide.

Zürcher Geschnetzeltes

Veal sliced thinly and served in a creamy white wine and mushroom sauce, traditionally from Zurich.

Lutefisk

A traditional Swiss fish dish, made by preserving cod in a lye solution and then rehydrating it before cooking.

Basler Läckerli

Traditional chewy biscuit from Basel, made with honey, almonds, and kirsch.

Älplermagronen

A hearty Swiss dish of pasta, potatoes, cheese, and cream, often served with applesauce.

Swiss Pastry

Delicate pastries such as Nusstorte, a nut-filled dessert popular in the Engadine region.

Swiss Muesli

Healthy breakfast dish made of rolled oats, fruit, nuts, and yogurt or milk.

Tartes des Alpes

Sweet Swiss tart made with alpine fruits such as apples and berries.

Raclette

A Swiss dish where melted cheese is scraped over potatoes, pickles, onions, and cured meats.

Chasselas Wine

Swiss white wine made from the Chasselas grape, often enjoyed with Swiss dishes like Fondue.

Malaysia

Nasi Lemak

Considered the national dish of Malaysia, Nasi Lemak consists of rice cooked in coconut milk, served with sambal, fried anchovies, hard-boiled egg, and cucumber.

Char Kway Teow

A popular stir-fried noodle dish made with flat rice noodles, prawns, eggs, and bean sprouts, cooked with a savory soy sauce.

Satay

Grilled skewers of marinated meat (usually chicken, beef, or lamb) served with a creamy peanut dipping sauce.

Laksa

A spicy noodle soup made with either a creamy coconut base or a tangy tamarind base, often served with shrimp, fish, or chicken.

Roti Canai

A flaky, crispy flatbread, often served with dhal (lentil curry) or a meat curry.

Hainanese Chicken Rice

A simple yet delicious dish of poached chicken served with fragrant rice cooked in chicken broth, along with chili and ginger sauce.

Mee Goreng Mamak

A stir-fried noodle dish with a unique blend of spices, tofu, vegetables, and optional meat, commonly served by Mamak stalls in Malaysia.

Nasi Goreng

Fried rice typically served with egg, vegetables, and sometimes meat or seafood, often accompanied by sambal or pickles.

Teh Tarik

Malaysia's beloved "pulled tea," made by pouring the tea back and forth between two containers to create a frothy texture.

Banana Leaf Rice

A traditional South Indian-inspired dish served on a banana leaf, usually consisting of rice, curry, vegetables, and papadum.

Kuih

Traditional Malaysian sweet and savory treats, often made from rice flour, coconut, or glutinous rice, and flavored with pandan or coconut milk.

Cendol

A refreshing dessert made with shaved ice, coconut milk, palm sugar, and green rice flour jelly, often enjoyed on hot days.
